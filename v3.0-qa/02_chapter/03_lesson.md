#Classes and IDs

Classes and IDs, also called **selectors**, are ways of targeting the style of specific HTML elements on your page.

So whatâ€™s the difference between them? In short, classes allow you to style many elements with a particular style, while IDs are only capable of styling a single element. Using these selectors allows you flexibility and control of styling individual, as well as groups, of elements on your page.
